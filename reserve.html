Reservation FAQ

We recommend booking at least 5 hours in advance for weekday dinners, and 12 hours for weekends and public holidays. For special occasions, private dining rooms, or large groups (8+ guests), please book at least 3–4 days in advance. Same-day reservations are accepted subject to availability — call us directly for the best chance.
Yes. You can modify or cancel your reservation up to 2 hours before the scheduled time by calling us or sending a WhatsApp message. We understand plans change — we just ask for reasonable notice so we can accommodate other guests. For same-day cancellations within 1 hour of the reservation, a courtesy call is appreciated.
Absolutely. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, jain, gluten-free, nut-free, and other dietary requirements. Please mention your needs in the "Special Requests" field when booking, and our kitchen team will prepare accordingly. For severe allergies, we recommend calling us directly so our staff can discuss your requirements personally.
Our Vizag branch features a private dining room that accommodates up to 20 guests, ideal for birthday dinners, anniversaries, business meetings, and party celebrations. The MVP branch also has a banquet hall for up to 20 guests. For private dining enquiries, please call +91 73311 44804.
We hold your reserved table for 15 minutes past the scheduled reservation time. If you are running late, please call us so we can hold the table longer. After 15 minutes without contact, we may need to release the table to walk-in guests, though we'll do our best to accommodate you when you arrive.
