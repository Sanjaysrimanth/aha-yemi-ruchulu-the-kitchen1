The Brand Story

More Than a Restaurant.
A Legacy of Flavour and Experience.

Aha Yemi Ruchulu was founded in Visakhapatnam with a simple idea — to make great food an experience that everyone can enjoy, regardless of taste or preference.

What began with a passion for quality cooking has grown into a multi-cuisine brand offering a wide range of flavours — from Indian favourites and Chinese dishes to pizzas, burgers, biryani, and more. Built on consistency, taste, and customer experience, Aha Yemi Ruchulu continues to grow as a trusted name in everyday dining.
